Empowering Opportunity

The Madison County Youth Court has created a disposition alternative that is aimed at helping juveniles in the justice system change the direction of their future. We call our project "Jobs4Youth" because a job unlocks a future that enables youth to live happy, healthy and productive lives.

What Do We Do?

Creating Community

We are hopeful that by redirecting youth towards the workforce while they are within the jurisdiction of the Youth Court, we can assist in minimizing the percentage of those who would have otherwise ended up in adult incarceration and subsequently increase the percentage of youth contributing to our community's workforce.
